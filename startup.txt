Startup Notes:
-------------
1. Upon seeing the OS verification screen, hold Ctrl+D down to boot to Chrome OS.

2. Use Ctrl+Alt+T to enter the shell.

3. Download Crouton
   >shell
   >sudo sh ~/Downloads/crouton -r trusty -t xfce
   
4. Switch between distros:
   sudo startxfce4 -n trusty (ubuntu 14.04)
or sudo startxfce4 -n precise (ubuntu 12.02)
